Comparison of .png and .jpg

Ways that .png is superior to .jpg

  • .pngs do not lose their quality from compression and don't become bluury or distorted no matter what you do
  • .pngs support transparency, so you can have different background for your image, text or logo
  • due to the fact that quality is not compromised, .pngs have sharper and crisper quality which is especially good for web graphics and logos

Ways .png is inferior to .jpg

  • .jpgs are smaller in file size, which might be more favourable
  • .jpgs are great for photographs as they support CMYK printing whereas .pngs are more for web usage
